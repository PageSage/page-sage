About

Our goal is to help book clubs select books that will be generally well received by the entire group. To achieve this, we ask our readers to rate twenty books based on how much they enjoyed their experiene with it, and will use this in a machine learning algorithm to gage how much that user may like other books. We'll average out the receptivity among all memembers in a bookclub to generate an overall rating. With Page Sage you will have your own personal alogrithm to help you estimate how much you may like a particular book, similar to Netflix's rating system.
